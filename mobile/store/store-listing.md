## English

**Name:** TwinMatch: AI Twin Dating
**Subtitle:**
Your AI twin does the small talk. You only show up for the right one.

**Description:**
TwinMatch flips the script on swipe fatigue. An AI digital twin that
learns your personality and communication style chats with compatible
candidates' twins on your behalf. When a genuine match signal emerges
and both sides consent, identities are revealed.

• Digital twin: an AI representative modeled on a short onboarding
• Twin chats: compatibility tested before you type a single message
• Compatibility report: communication, lifestyle, values and goals
• Consent-gated reveal: your profile stays hidden until mutual approval
• Safe dating: vetted venue suggestions and a date planner
• Gift system: send surprises without sharing your address

Privacy first: location is used only for nearby venue suggestions and
photos stay hidden until reveal.

For ages 18 and up.
